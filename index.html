A NEW ERA OF WEARABLE GAMING

A Competitive Wearable Multiplayer Game

Made in CS 377W (HCI Issues in Wearable Computing), Glassassin is a glass based multiplayer chase game. A hunter is assigned a target to capture within a given time limit - and both players see each other's video feed!

Design & Research Paper

This is an academic paper describing our research while building Glassassin. It covers what we worked on, why it's important, previous related work, our user-centered design methodology, the decisions we made, the system we built along with all our learning and outcomes of this project. There is also a presentation, which was delivered in class, that outlines the previous research work as well as our HCI process through the project.
